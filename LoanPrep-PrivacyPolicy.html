LOANPREP PRIVACY POLICY

Your Privacy Matters

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Overview

LoanPrep ("the App", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Important Notice

LoanPrep is a local tool application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

Key Principles

  • Your data stays on your device
  • No cloud storage of personal information
  • No tracking or profiling
  • Full control over your data
  • Transparent about what we collect

Information We Collect

LoanPrep operates locally on your device. We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

Assessment Information

  • Loan amount and purpose
  • Monthly income
  • Employment status and duration
  • Credit history category
  • Existing debts
  • Collateral type and value (if provided)

App Usage Data (Stored Locally)

  • Points balance and transaction history
  • Daily check-in streaks
  • Checklist items and progress
  • Assessment results and history
  • App preferences (theme, currency, notifications)

Purchase Information

  • Product purchased (single report or package)
  • Number of reports included and used
  • Apple transaction ID and product ID
  • Purchase date
What We DON'T Collect
  • Your name or email address
  • Contact information
  • Precise location data
  • Photos or media
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Browsing history

How We Use Your Information

All Processing is Local

All data processing happens locally on your device. No information is sent to external servers.

Provide Core Features

  • Generate loan preparation assessment reports
  • Create preparation checklists based on your inputs
  • Track your progress and activities
  • Manage points and daily check-in features
  • Store your preferences and settings

Fulfill Purchases

  • Track available report credits
  • Maintain purchase history locally
  • Verify entitlements for paid features

We Do NOT Use Your Information For

  • Advertising or marketing profiling
  • Sharing with third parties
  • Cross-app tracking
  • Building user profiles on servers
  • Any purpose beyond core functionality

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and local JSON files). Your data never leaves your device except as described below for purchases.

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your iOS device may automatically backup LoanPrep data as part of your device backup. You can manage this in your iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Security Measures

  • We use iOS platform security features
  • Data is protected by your device security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • No cloud servers storing your personal information
  • Regular security reviews and updates
Your Responsibility
  • Keep your device secure with a strong passcode
  • Enable Face ID or Touch ID
  • Keep iOS updated
  • Be cautious when entering sensitive information

Data Sharing & Transfers

We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do not share your data with third parties, data brokers, or advertising networks.

Apple In-App Purchases

When you purchase reports through the App Store, Apple processes the payment and provides us with limited transaction information (transaction ID, product ID) necessary to verify your purchase. Apple's Privacy Policy governs their handling of payment information.

No Third-Party Services

  • No analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No data brokers or aggregators
  • No cloud storage services for user data

International Transfers

Because all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers of your personal information.

Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data. Depending on your location, you may have legal rights including:

Access & Control

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Remove all data via Profile → Data Management → Delete All Data
  • Export: Contact us for data export assistance
  • Correct: Edit your information directly in the app

California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

European Users (GDPR)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
How to Exercise Your Rights

Use the in-app Delete All Data feature or contact us at ByteDanceDev@outlook.com for assistance.

Children's Privacy

Age Restriction

LoanPrep is not directed to children under 13 years of age (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction, such as 16 in the EEA).

Our Commitment

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We do not market to children
  • The app is designed for adult users preparing for loans

Parental Notice

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please use the in-app Delete All Data feature and contact us immediately at ByteDanceDev@outlook. com.

Users under 18 should use this app only with parental or guardian involvement.

Advertising & Tracking

No Advertising or Tracking

LoanPrep does NOT include third-party advertising networks, user tracking, cross-app tracking, behavioral advertising, or ad display functionality.

Current Status

The current version does not contain advertising features or integrations. All functionality operates locally without external ad services.
