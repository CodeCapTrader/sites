Privacy-First Financial Tools

We prioritize your privacy. Core app data—like bills, splits, and personal notes—is stored locally on your device; the app does not require an account or automatically upload your financial entries to our servers. To keep the app free, we use Google AdMob to show ads; AdMob and its partners may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to deliver and measure ads. The app itself does not share your personal bills or notes with third parties.
