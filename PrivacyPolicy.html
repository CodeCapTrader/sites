Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Your privacy is our top priority. All our apps store data locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our financial applications ("we", "our", or "the apps") handle your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our applications.

Information Collection and Use

What We DON'T Collect

Our apps are designed with privacy-first principles. We do NOT:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools

Local Data Storage

All data you enter into our apps is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Financial goals and targets
  • Transaction records
  • Income and expense entries
  • Budget analytics and reports
  • Custom categories and tags
  • App preferences and settings

Data Security

Your data security is paramount to us:

  • Local Storage Only: All data remains on your device
  • No Internet Transfer: Your financial data is never transmitted over the internet
  • Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • Future Updates: Enhanced security features may be added in future updates

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device:

  • Your app data may be included in your device's iCloud backup
  • This backup is encrypted and managed by Apple
  • You can disable app backup in your device's iCloud settings if preferred
  • We have no access to your iCloud backups

Third-Party Services

Our apps do NOT use any third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • User tracking
  • Data processing

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data directly within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete any or all data instantly
  • Export: Export features may be available in future updates

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The apps are intended for general audiences interested in personal finance management.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the apps after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
