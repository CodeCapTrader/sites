Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 4, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies globally to Smart Loan Payoff Calculator (the "App"). The App runs entirely on-device. We do not upload your entered data (e.g., loan amounts, payment scenarios, calculations) to our servers.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect directly identifying information such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Payment information or financial account details
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Precise location data
  • Device identifiers for cross-app tracking

We do not upload your input data from the App to our servers, including:

  • Loan amounts and balances
  • Interest rates and payment details
  • Prepayment scenarios and calculations
  • Saved scenarios and comparison results
  • Any notes or custom data you enter

On-Device Processing and Storage

All your financial data and calculations are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and local storage.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • All calculations are performed locally
  • No cloud synchronization or backup to our servers
  • Data is only accessible on your device

Your data remains private and under your control at all times.

Data Sharing

Content is only shared when you actively use the system share functionality:

  • Using iOS share sheet to export calculations or screenshots
  • Exporting payment schedules as PDF or CSV files

In these cases, Apple's and the receiving third party's terms and privacy policies apply. We have no control over how third-party apps or services handle shared data.

We do not automatically share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Third-Party Policies and Responsibility

When you:

  • Open external links (e.g., App Store for ratings/reviews)
  • Use third-party services or apps to receive shared data
  • Access external websites from the App

Those third parties may collect or process information under their own policies. Please review and comply with the respective terms and privacy policies of Apple, and any third-party apps or websites you interact with.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it:

  • Delete individual scenarios or comparisons within the App
  • Use "Delete All Data" in Settings to clear all saved data
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data

Important: When you delete data or uninstall the App, all locally stored information is permanently removed from your device. This action cannot be undone.

We do not retain copies of your data on any servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please:

  • Contact us immediately at the email address below
  • Delete the App and all associated data from the device

Parents and guardians should monitor their children's use of mobile applications.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometrics)
  • No data transmission to external servers means no risk of network interception
  • The App uses iOS sandbox security to isolate data from other apps

However, no method of storage is 100% secure. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling biometric authentication
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals

International Data Transfers

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, there are no international data transfers to our servers.

If you use the system share feature to send data to third-party services, those services may process data in different countries under their own privacy policies.
