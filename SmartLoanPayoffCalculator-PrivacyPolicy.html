Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies globally to Smart Loan Payoff Calculator (the "App"). The App runs entirely on-device. We do not upload your entered data (e.g., loan amounts, payment scenarios, calculations) to our servers.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. We only serve non-personalized ads that do not use your personal data for ad targeting. Please see the "Advertising" section below for detailed information.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect directly identifying information such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Payment information or financial account details
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Precise location data
  • Device identifiers for cross-app tracking

We do not upload your input data from the App to our servers, including:

  • Loan amounts and balances
  • Interest rates and payment details
  • Prepayment scenarios and calculations
  • Saved scenarios and comparison results
  • Any notes or custom data you enter

On-Device Processing and Storage

All your financial data and calculations are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and local storage.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • All calculations are performed locally
  • No cloud synchronization or backup to our servers
  • Data is only accessible on your device
  • We do NOT use your data for AI training, machine learning, or algorithm development
  • We do NOT share your data with any third parties for research or analytics purposes

Your data remains private and under your control at all times.

Advertising and Non-Personalized Ads

The App displays advertisements to support free access to all features. We partner with Google AdMob to serve ads.

IMPORTANT: We Only Use Non-Personalized Ads

  • We do NOT serve personalized (targeted) ads
  • Ads are NOT based on your personal information, interests, or behavior
  • We do NOT track your activity across apps or websites for advertising
  • We do NOT create advertising profiles about you

Non-personalized ads are based on:

  • Contextual information (the content you're currently viewing)
  • General location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Time of day and language settings

Information Collected by Google AdMob:

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain technical information to serve and measure ads:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • Ad interaction data: Whether an ad was viewed or clicked
  • General location: Approximate location based on IP address (country/city level only)
  • Technical identifiers: IP address, advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • App usage: App name, App Store ID, session information

This information is used solely for:

  • Serving non-personalized ads appropriate to your region
  • Measuring ad performance and detecting fraud
  • Complying with legal requirements

Your Advertising Identifier (IDFA):

iOS provides an advertising identifier (IDFA) that can be used for advertising purposes. Even though we only serve non-personalized ads, AdMob may collect this identifier for fraud prevention and measurement.

You can control IDFA usage:

  • Go to iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Or, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Toggle off "Personalized Ads"

For more information about Google's advertising practices, please review:

  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob's Privacy Information: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
  • How Google uses data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data Sharing

Your loan calculation data is never shared with third parties. Content is only shared when you actively use the system share functionality:

  • Using iOS share sheet to export calculations or screenshots
  • Exporting payment schedules as PDF or CSV files

Advertising Partners:

We share limited technical information with Google AdMob for the sole purpose of serving non-personalized ads and measuring ad performance. We do NOT share your financial data, loan information, or any personal calculation details with advertising partners.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your Financial Data:

Your loan calculation data remains on your device until you choose to delete it:

  • Delete individual scenarios or comparisons within the App
  • Use "Delete All Data" in Settings to clear all saved data
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data

When you delete data or uninstall the App, all locally stored information is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometrics)
  • No data transmission to external servers means no risk of network interception
  • The App uses iOS sandbox security to isolate data from other apps
